School History

GS Bumba TSS is located in Rutsiro District in the Western Province of Rwanda. The school was originally established as a general education Groupe Scolaire. Between 2011 and 2013 the school was integrated into the Technical Secondary School (TSS) / TVET education system to strengthen technical training.

In 2020 the institution was formally re-accredited to provide Level 3–5 technical programs, helping students gain practical skills and professional knowledge required in the labor market and higher education.

Trades We Offer

  • Accounting (Y1 – Y3)
  • Computer Systems (L3 – L5)
  • Software Development (L3 – L5)
